== Description ==

Themebeez Social Feed is a simple WordPress plugin that helps you display your Instagram feeds on your website. You add your Instagram feed onto your website either with a widget or shortcode.

== How to use? ==

**As Widget**

* Navigate to Appearance > Widgets.
* Drag and drop **TSF: Instagram Widget** into the desired widget area.
* Expand the **TSF: Instagram Widget** to configure settings.
* Once configured, save the settings.

**As Shortcode**

Copy and paste the shortcode, [themebeez-social-feed], in your post/page.

* `nums` - Number of photos. Maximum 30
* `cols` - Photos per row. Maximum 6
* `displaycaption` - Whether to display photo's caption
* `displayprofilelink` - Whether to display button to link Instagram account
* `buttontitle` - Title of button to link Instagram account
* `cssclass` - Add a class to feed container

Example

`[themebeez-social-feed nums="10" cols="6" displaycaption=true displayprofilelink=true buttontitle="Follow Me" cssclass="test-class custom-class"]`


== Installation ==

1. Navigate to **Plugins** on your dashboard.
2. Click on **Add News** button.
3. In plugin search box, search for **Themebeez Social Feed** and the plugin will be listed.
4. Click on **Install Now** button to install the plugin.
5. Once installation is completed click on **Activate** button to activate the plugin.
6. On you dashboard, click on **TB Social Feed** and connect your Instagram account.


== FAQs ==

= How to connect to Instagram account? =

On your dashboard, click on **TB Social Feed**. Then under **Connect An Instagram Account** section, click on button to connect your Instagram account.

= What to do when you get invalid access token error? =

If you are getting this error on your WordPress dashbord, then you need to reconnet to your Instagram account. Because, access token expires from time to time.
